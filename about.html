About Marian Hotel

Marian Hotel is an absolute independent boutique hotel located in the heart of Calabar. We offer modern comfort blended with the rich culture of Sub-Saharan Africa. Located at #125 Old Ikang Rd, Big Qua Town, Calabar 540281, Cross River , the hotel offers secure and comfortable lodging for visitors and business travelers alike.
